About the Summer Solstice Music Festival

Help raise money to find a cure for Cystic Fibrosis!

Buzz and Jrray (aka Kim and Jerry) have been hosting Summer Solstice since 1991 to benefit the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation.

Solstice is an all day / night family-friendly music festival. Come on out to see old friends and make new ones, support live local music and celebrate the start of summer!
